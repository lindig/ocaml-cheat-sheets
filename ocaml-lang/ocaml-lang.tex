files.\\
 Comments can be nested, between delimiters \verb!(*!...\verb!*)!\\
Integers: \verb!123!, \verb!1_000!, \verb!0x4533!, \verb!0o773!, \verb!0b1010101!\\
Chars: \verb!'a'!, \verb!'\255'!, \verb!'\xFF'!, \verb!'\n'!
